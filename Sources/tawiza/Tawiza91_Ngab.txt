 uïïun 91, yemrayur 2004 ( 2004) Tamazight Tanut n awal Yura t: Tarwa n tmura nnes (Libya) tanut tetcar s awal, tetcar s tedvsva s fad n tira zedghegh dis wil ufegh tmedjet tesal urigh s imettvawen n udergal asefu d ulawen zrigh udan di terkas ssawen tessun agar yimlu d imal tazlun mrawen d wil usedc ass amelal. ill ad kerzagh askel ad rzagh d ass ad tegh arra n tmura nnu tmetent ad esghagh. ugdagh s uta n unzvar gdagh s tilli s tedvsva n memmi tidit suigh ses d ughegh tekrest aypwa af adrar ad azlun usan n tafsuit tbucelt ad t zragh tselut ghesa d udem nnu ad t gar slan asali tufut d ayugur atan d ayased asafar. ill ad kerzahg askel ad rzagh d ass ad tegh arra n tmura nnu tmetent ad sghagh.
