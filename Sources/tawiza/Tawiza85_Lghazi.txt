 Uttvun 85, Semyur 2004 (Mai 2004) Tamazight Asounda Yaru t: Lakbir Lghazi (aghbalu n tsrdan) Awa ad k ctix a yass a daw wussann, awa a yass nna g iggudi mcrrdvul, awa llig imtti Rif ig amm taffa awa a yaraw n uxttvabi tsmun agh awa mecta agg uderr apa ur immut awa aggan agujil zeg ta awa ayimz da gh axub ismun agh awa winna immuten tuggah kuci awa isul ad ikk nni gagh wacal awa winna ioman d widda rzvanin awa illan g jaj n txamin awa winna gher walu ma g tghiman awa ad ex tuger ta di gh ti awa tenna g illa uhmadju n lofit awa soundax kwen a yimazighen n Rif awa zeg nnig n fazaz ad zeg sghuyyux awa lla ttinix a yimazighen inu awa awa gg ufan ann aggin ghurun awa ayaraw n uxttvabi tsmunagh awa murid is ad gh t id ikjem ca awa ann asix aoggadi xef ighir awa maca t kkad nnig agh titi awa kuyuk ig a yimazighen afus awa a yaytma onda d ismun agh.
