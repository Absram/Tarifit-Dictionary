 uïïun 96, kuçyur 2005 (Avril 2005) Tamazight Tanfust taqudat: Taghyuri!! yura t: saoid belgharbi Tighit axmi d ttass ghar utarras tennay d x wafriwen n idbaren icemraren. Maca tibbuperya ttass d x idvaren nnes, war d tennay ura x weghyur. Deg ukud a ra d ighyar ggin min xafsen, axmi temsagaren ik tibbuperya deg abrid tagin ad tejjen ad tni ura x zoenquq nsen, ra d nitni tudf iten tehraymect, snayen ghar wi yedjan nsen, mara war dayek ufin tiznaf nsen war cek tejjin ad xafsen tbaoared idvaren nnec. Mara tetvfed kidsen taghennant ad tafed ixef nnec tessud ca n weqwir s ca n arecret, nigh ad tafed ticedvyin nsen d tixrazvin deg imezvughen nnec!!! Ikkar weghyur acemrar yenna s i wmas abarcan: -Kkar ad neg ixef nnegh neprec, neprura, war ntet war nwarred, ad nexzvar min ghar yeg radj n taddart!?? Ishundart weghyur nni abarcan s dehhact, arami d as d wdvan irizazen di rmedwed, uca yarra yenna s : -d ta umi qqaren taghyuri, ad qqimegh s bupeyyuf d fad huma ad xafi icetv weomud, ad dafegh ixef inu di barra, tazeddight walu, amecri d umensi walu, min yewca arebbi d wa d netta wapa!!!!! -Zeoma amenni ixsegh, arezzugh x tirelli inu, ad duyaregh mani mma xsegh d mri mma xsegh, war tghimigh kessigh dedj nsen, refyafi ccuren s ighedj n imendi..., war zemmaregh ad qqimegh d anehbus n ssrimet d tbarda d igharynen, mri mma n pezzen ad rzun x weghyur, nec war dji min ghar ggegh s tudart am taniti, ad rezzugh war tesrigh i ca ura d rra, atarras ad yegg ixef nnes iprec hsen, huma ad t’ ndvaren ghar bara. -Maca cek d aghyur war tedjid ca d atarras?? - Dij, am weghyur am utarras, marra idnegh nemsarwas, tesmun anegh taghyuri, mri rad n taddart itaca minzi n taca neccin iri yewca negh tasghart nnegh, umi adji nnes dayes taghyuri yettu yanegh arami d anegh yajja n txarrvas ad nedha d imeprac. Maghar yadja ca n weghyur nigh ca nebnadem yarezzu reprac i yexf nnes!!. (saoid belgharbi, anebdu n 2003) Amazigh31@hotmail.com 2002 Tawiza. .
