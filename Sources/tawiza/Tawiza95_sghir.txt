 uïïun 95, krayur 2005 (Mars 2005) Tamazight azerf inu Yura t: Sghir Oumar Azerf inu yusi t wasif ineggin igg ur yigh aregaz ioazzan yusi t ulxix s imedwan azerf inu yuyella s iggenwan igg ur yigh ayedvidv ittaylalen yusi t uzewu s ghur iteran azerf inu izela y iberdan igg ur svebiregh i yexlalen yusi t wanna ghefes izerin azerf inu umzen t wuccan igg ur ggigh anegmar ineqqan yusi t leghder s gar iferan. Ad zegergh asif ineggin yegh aydvidv ittaylalen amezegh iberdan lsegh idukan kecemegh s iferan nnba yillasen ak inniyegh a yazerf inu aked amzegh s ufus inu raregh kid s uxam inu zzughek ammas n igger inu (Sghir oumar, ass n: 03/11/04, asif n dades) 2002 Tawiza. .
