 imettvawen n yidv Yaru t: Oumar Sghir annayegh t... yuylla s iggnwan yuylla igga wam uzwu igga wam usem ur ttannay titv. zerigh t... iggellin itters itters f tenmmutert ar inedder. Tanadra nnes... asafar i wanna zares isefliden temaned d imettvawen imettvawen n yidv. izelay as ubrid... iggellin ur issin mani yetka ar ittannay adrar lilas ar isuggur s wasi tama nes. Mek yuylla s ighuliden... tandra nnes ur sar as nesella mek iman d wasif imettvawen nnes ur sar ten nzri. Iqquma diyi iggellin... amzegh t gger ifasen yerzvem iyefrawen nnes acku ur diggi yannay timitar n uneggmar. usegh t... sersert aflla n ighir inu awigh t ar yat tebadut nnigh as: mun d ubrid degh qan taft aynna ttinigget. Ighezzif ubrid nnes... iggellin ur iri ayezpu ajmil nna yas yigh yuyella ittverd dis yan ufraw zund iyezri mas tid kettir. Tejra dis iggelllin... idda ur dyaghul tejra diyi awd nekkin ddigh ursar t zrigh. Ighulegh d... ar ghifes seqsagh righ inermisen nnes sellagh ima di yennan iwaliwen bbin ul inu. Pan wigh... teggit aflla n ighir tazent s tvartv ighuliden itteggras heyayyu yawi t s yer ighuliden izred ddis idamen izuggaren. Gar ussan... gar inermisen sellagh asen tvergh amezgh akal nnigh as: imkennagh ar ijran y tudert nesseggma uccan tecnagh nerur i tillas tvefurnt agh. Ur ta nelli... is nesul a nilli? nesul numezv tillas wa manik a ya sid mani yikka wayur mani yitran n yidv mani tudert n yan izelan. (sghir oumar di agadir, assen 21/11/04)
