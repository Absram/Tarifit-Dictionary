 uïïun 97, smyur 2005 (Mai 2005) Tamazight Agerram g umadval "John paul" wis sin ixsi (1920-2005) Yura t: Bucxar Hemmu Ass n 2/4/05 ixsi itvij n idj ugerram axatar g umerwas (religion) n ikatulikn. Ikker d g umerret , d tlufa n tghewwaght takweswat tisnat. Inwer i yghewwaghen n Pitlir (Hitlir). Ffir, mag yiwetv 38 useggas yufu ixf nnes la itari timedyazin, d tizra, tabrat n tghuri yura t xef: St jen la croix, ametru n Krakuvi ighrem manig imgher, issufegh d u ict n tezra “essai” ilagh as: tayri d twuri. Kkarul fritiwa, ara y gen Jean Paul2. G useggas 1966, llama n tmurt n Bolonya (Pologne), manig ilul, x 1000 useggas tessek latezdi, inna g mag sselmed, is irvzvem x mayellan g tudert, d tukra (defendre) x imezvladv, nta ag nnan “kem it zeglex, kenni ara it isemqaddan”, awal inna s taynert ur ikerricen. xsex btvaytvay. itenbadvin n umadval, d iwdan, issghuy:ur teggidem rezvmat azilal i Jessus, ad ken yawetv usgunfu d utverreh. Yiwi t lhal ad ikkes ag ayeddan yixf (propriété privée), d kem it igru, iberremgher iwdan ad sen isselmed: ur t adrat iy ifassen nnun, gat ayen issidden datawit. Tawriwin, d izetvwan iggar manig tell can temnatv, tiwy as d g ca n twala, ict n tmenghiwt x ufus n idj uturki, las tlaghan: Oli Aksa. lqertvas issufegh degs, betvren sis gher ssbitvar, day inwer iwkus (mort) ur ixsi, ul nnes ttughet d akeswat, yaghul ig yiwetv, ghers g tesraft manig it qqnen, da ayen myannan iqqim, jarat sen urilli may t issnen. Titiyu tedda s d, is yamu g ayt tawada n uswiz (marche de solidarite) ara y ssilin Lic valisa, g 1987, gher tenbadvt n tmurt. Ur teqqim xas i yzeggaghen (communiste) g ufus taghul d ilan jar mag llan g tmurt n tmesmunin d lghaci. G t ixita ag lagha x ussbed n trvzvi (destruction). Is iorem x tilelli n Jzssus, d uorvtv (prière)  d idj n azul i Mari (dévotion).
