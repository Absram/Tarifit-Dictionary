Ass u yetbeddel lhal lzven ijerren d idan In tusa d isan Mnit, mnit n azal ad tawid agar itvan tuh niyyet tagara nnek kan. Sesten iman nnek apat tmeddert ad ttuklal ad temmedder war imal waqila yemmet ul nnek addew negh nneg ical ammek ad yaghyi dek awal.
