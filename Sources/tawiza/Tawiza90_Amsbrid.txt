 uïïun 90, mrayur 2004 (Octobre 2004) Tamazight Antun n tirra Yura t: Mimun AMSBRID (Biljik) "La poésie est indispensable, mais je ne sais pas à quoi" (Jean Cocteau) Yetwanna yi: ur ttarrid X wazvri d tmgharin, X tayri, Xf iyezzimen nnec D tghufawin. X uwddvar D imettvawen. X tirra nnec i farghen, X ybriden war itiwdven, X ticri di test, X wttvu n ifadden. Xf inuraz yuyren, X wur yedjuzen… Xafek cek: X wi tedjid? Mays d tusid? Mani teddud? May tarezzud? Mani tedjid?… Nnigh as: arzu xafi deg idvffisen n tirra, Jar wawaren, Jar useghdi d usiwel, Deg ineggifen n yzvri, Di min yessghad, D min itini. Iman, tayri, azvri_ D nitni d antun n yzri. Antun ur yetghimi imun!: Yetwagw deg warcti, Ifessi days, Iticca s tujjut nnes; Zzay s i ytinbi, Xafs i yetwacc. 2002 Tawiza. .
